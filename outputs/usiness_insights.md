# Business Insights

## 1. Sales Trend

**Observation**

Sales fluctuate throughout the year with noticeable seasonal patterns.

**Evidence**

Monthly sales trend visualization.

**Business Interpretation**

Demand varies by season and promotional periods.

**Recommendation**

Increase inventory and marketing before high-demand periods.

---

## 2. Regional Performance

**Observation**

Some regions generate significantly higher revenue and profit.

**Evidence**

Regional performance dashboard.

**Business Interpretation**

Regional market performance differs considerably.

**Recommendation**

Replicate successful regional strategies in lower-performing markets.

---

## 3. Category Profitability

**Observation**

Certain product categories produce strong revenue but lower profitability.

**Evidence**

Category Profitability chart.

**Business Interpretation**

High sales alone do not guarantee profitability.

**Recommendation**

Review pricing strategy and product costs.

---

## 4. Customer Segment Behaviour

**Observation**

Customer segments contribute differently to revenue.

**Evidence**

Customer Segment comparison.

**Business Interpretation**

Different customer groups have unique purchasing patterns.

**Recommendation**

Develop targeted marketing campaigns for each segment.

---

## 5. Discount Impact

**Observation**

Higher discounts generally reduce profitability.

**Evidence**

Discount vs Profit scatter plot.

**Business Interpretation**

Excessive discounting negatively affects business margins.

**Recommendation**

Introduce discount thresholds and promotional policies.

---

## 6. Shipping Performance

**Observation**

Longer delivery times are associated with poorer operational performance.

**Evidence**

Shipping Performance chart.

**Business Interpretation**

Delivery efficiency directly impacts customer experience.

**Recommendation**

Improve logistics planning and optimize shipping methods.

---

## 7. Return Analysis

**Observation**

Returns are concentrated within specific categories.

**Evidence**

Return Analysis dashboard.

**Business Interpretation**

Product quality or customer expectation issues may exist.

**Recommendation**

Investigate product defects and improve product descriptions.

---

## 8. Business Risk

**Observation**

High discounts combined with high return rates reduce profitability.

**Evidence**

Dashboard KPIs and multiple visualizations.

**Business Interpretation**

Revenue growth without profit growth is unsustainable.

**Recommendation**

Focus on profitable growth rather than increasing sales volume alone.

---

# Calculated Fields Used

* Profit Margin = Profit / Sales
* Cost = Sales − Profit
* Average Order Value = Sales / Distinct Orders
* Return Rate = Returned Orders / Total Orders
* Shipping Delay Bucket = Fast, Normal and Delayed delivery categories

These calculated fields support executive-level performance monitoring and business decision-making.
